Rough meaning of structures used
---
P: pre-calculation settings
Q: Qsmr settings  
R: repository for internal variables
S: system settings 

S and R are basically only for internal use. P not used when doing inversions.


To start up Qsmr
---
q2_init;


For automatic check of Q, do
---
Q = q_std(1);    
q2_check_q( Q );
